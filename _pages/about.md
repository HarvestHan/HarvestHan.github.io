I am a Master's graduate student at the College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Zhengzhou University. My research focuses on **information bottleneck** and **multi-view clustering**, with recent work extending into single-cell RNA-seq data analysis. I look forward to engaging in academic exchanges with those who share similar research interests. You can reach me via email at <strong>iefshan@gs.zzu.edu.cn</strong>.

My research interests include:
- Information Bottleneck Theory
- Multi-view Clustering
- Self-supervised Learning
- Single-cell RNA-seq Analysis
